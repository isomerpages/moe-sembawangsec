respectful communication, collective effort, and timely engagement, we
create a collaborative environment that ensures every student receives
the support they need to achieve their full potential.</p>

<h4>Communication Hours</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents are advised to contact our teachers during school hours, <strong>7:30 am to 5:00 pm, Monday to Friday, during term time</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Other than in emergencies, staff are not required to respond outside school
hours or during school holidays.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For <strong>urgent matters</strong>, please contact the <strong>General Office (6756 6760)</strong> during
school operating hours. Our teachers may be in class or unavailable.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Teachers’ Contact Information</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please note that teachers are not obliged to share their personal mobile
numbers, and your understanding is appreciated.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Response Time</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The school aims to provide responses within:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>3 working days:</strong> For simple enquiries</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>7 working days:</strong> For enquiries or cases requiring investigation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>21 working days:</strong> For complex enquiries or cases involving
multiple parties or external agencies.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

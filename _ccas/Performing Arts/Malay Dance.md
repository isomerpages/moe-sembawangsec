<hr>
<p>The Sembawang Malay Dance have achieved numerous accolades since its establishment.
The dance club has received Gold/Gold with Honours/Certificate of Distinction
in the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Central Judging from 2003 to 2019.
The dance club has also staged local public performance at the Esplanade
and Ngee Ann City and performed overseas in Bandung, (Indonesia), Kuala
Lumpur (Malaysia) and Ho Chi Minh City(Vietnam).</p>
<p>Their weekly training takes place at 2.30pm – 5.30pm.</p>
<p>The dance club also have enrichment programmes, local and overseas learning
journeys to experience other cultures.</p>
<p>Our Malay Dancers was given the opportunity to further enhance their skills
and knowledge of other cultural dance technique from our overseas immersion
programmes to Bandung, Indonesia (2011), Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia (2017)
and Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam (2019).</p>
<p>We also have the privilege to be invited annually to perform at Esplanade
for the Muara Festival since 2012.</p>

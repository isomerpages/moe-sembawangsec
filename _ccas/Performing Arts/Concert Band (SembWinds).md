<p>Sembawang Secondary School's Concert Band is a vibrant hub for students
to cultivate their passion for music. The band not only focuses on honing
skills in various instruments but also provides a dynamic platform for
students to showcase their musical talents. Through dedicated training
sessions and performances, the Concert Band at Sembawang Secondary School
serves as a catalyst for nurturing musical abilities, fostering a love
for music, and creating memorable experiences for both participants and
audiences alike. Through participation in Singapore Youth Festival, the
Band has attained Accomplishment status for the year, 2021 and 2023</p>
<h3>Programmes:</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Participation in Singapore Youth Festival&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weekly Practice&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Band Major and Drum Major Workshop&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exchange Programmes with various schools&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>

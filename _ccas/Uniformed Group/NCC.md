#### **OUR BELIEF**

The Vikings symbolize our aspirations of our cadets:

1.  To be disciplined
2.  To be fearless to explore the unknown
3.  To be united and support each other – as a brotherhood, as a family

In the Sembawang Vikings Family, our cadets aspire to be a family that is strong, disciplined and close-knitted.

### **OUR ACTIVITIES**

**Fitness and Military knowledge**

*   Fitness Competition, Mini League
*   Individual Field Craft
*   Speech Day Parades, National Day parades, SAF Day parades
*   Precision (Rifle) Drills and Freestyle Drills
*   Technical Handing and Live Firing
*   Navy Ship & Base Visits

![](/images/NCC2.png)

**Adventure**

*   Camp FORGE & Camp STEEL: High element, Low element, Flying Fox, Rappelling, Paintball
*   School Camp: Bubble soccer, Archery, Field cooking, Road March
*   Overseas Expedition: Mount Ophir Expedition, International Sea Cadet Exchange Programme

![](/images/NCC3.png)

**Water Sports**

*   1 Star and 2 Star Kayaking Course
*   Annual Kayaking Expeditions
*   Dragon-Boating and Sea Competition

![](/images/NCC4.png)

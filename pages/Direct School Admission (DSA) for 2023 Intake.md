# Direct School Admission (DSA) Exercise for 2023 Intake

Sembawang Secondary School (SMB) invites all current P6 students to apply for admission to our school in the academic year of 2023 through the **Direct School Admission (DSA)** exercise.

**Before applying, please READ the following:**

1.  Students successfully allocated to their DSA-Sec schools will **NOT** be allowed to participate in the annual S1 Posting Exercise to opt for secondary schools, and are expected to honour the commitment to the allocated DSA-Sec school for the entire duration of the programme.
2. They will also **NOT** be allowed to transfer to another school after the release of the PSLE results.
3. All places offered through this DSA exercise are conditional and subject to the student’s eligibility for an academic course offered by the school i.e. Express, Normal (Academic) or Normal (Technical) based on their PSLE results.

An applicant can indicate up to three choices (maximum of **two choices for the same school** under two different talent areas). **The closing date for all DSA applications is** **Wednesday, 01 June 2022 (3.00 p.m)**

#### **(B) Application Process**

Centralised DSA-Sec Application Process

Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal. The application is free-of-charge and will be open from **05 May 2022 to 01 June 2022**. For more details, please refer to MOE website: [**www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec**](http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec)

#### **(C) Selection Process**

All candidates are shortlisted and will be selected based on merit (performance during trials/auditions and interviews) The DSA selection panel will ensure that the selection process is objective, transparent and merit-based. Please note that meeting all criteria does not guarantee the student will be shortlisted/given an offer.

For **Sports and Games** CCA:

*   Applicants are to attend at least one trial in July 2022.

For **Performing Arts** CCA:

*   Applicants are to attend an audition in July 2022.

For **Leadership and Uniformed Groups**:

*   Applicants are to attend an interview in July 2022.

For **Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics:**

*   Applicants are to attend an interview in July 2022.

#### **(E) Important Information**

*   **Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop the area of talent.**
*   General academic ability tests such as the General Ability Test (**GAT**) and Higher Ability Selection Test (**HAST**) are **NOT** required for the selection process.
*   Parents may log in directly to DSA-Sec Portal using your SingPass. Parents/ guardians who are unable to apply online can approach your child’s primary school for assistance.
*   Applicants **not** studying in mainstream MOE primary schools are required to apply for a Registration number (RGxxxxxxx) before they can apply through the DSA-Sec Portal. Refer to the MOE website [https://go.gov.sg/nmsdsa-sec-application](https://go.gov.sg/nmsdsa-sec-application) for more details.
*   All International Students are required to take part in the Admissions Exercise for International Students (**AEIS**) before they are considered for DSA-Sec allocation.
*   For more information on DSA, please visit [**moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec**](http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec)

<h4><strong>Release of GCE O Level Examination Results 2024</strong></h4>
<p>The 2024 O Level results will be released on 10 January 2025, Friday.</p>
<p>Students from Sec 4D, Sec 4E, Sec 5A and students from Sec 4A, 4B and
4C, who have sat for papers at the O Level Exam, are to report to the school
hall by 2.15pm on 10 January 2025 to attend an important briefing regarding
post-secondary courses before collecting your result slips and certificates.</p>
<p>Please report in full school uniform or PE T-shirt and trousers/skirt
and be in proper grooming. If you are feeling unwell on 10 January 2025,
do not report to school. Inform your teachers and they will provide you
with more information.</p>
<p>Parents/Guardians can refer to the attached message and information sheet
on how you can better support your child/ward.</p>

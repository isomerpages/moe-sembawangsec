<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PE2.png"></div><p><strong>About the Department</strong></p><p>The department aims for Holistic Outcomes through Physical Education (HOPE) and focuses on developing pupils’ games skills, nurturing sportsmanship values and equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and attitudes to pursue and enjoy a physically active and healthy lifestyle.</p><p><strong>Learning Opportunities for Students</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Inter House Games</strong></p></li></ul><p>A series of sports competitions for our students to establish new friendships and demonstrate their learning.</p><ul><li><p><strong>SMB Youth Run</strong></p></li></ul><p>The Mass Run aims to instill joy, a love for the outdoors and resilience amongst our students. Through these platforms our students become active and learn to inculcate healthy lifestyle.</p><ul><li><p><strong>SMB Youth Games</strong></p></li></ul><p>Our school’s sports and games carnival where students apply their learning from PE lessons. It is also a day to promote fun, adopt healthy habits and develop character such as excellence and sportsmanship values.</p><ul><li><p><strong>Outdoor Adventure Learning Camps</strong></p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Sec 1 Camp Resilience&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Secondary 3 MOE-OBS Challenge Programme&nbsp;</p></li></ul><p>Our Outdoor Education is weaved into our residential camps as they provide authentic learning experiences for our students to develop appropriate outdoor skills while cultivating an attitude of care and appreciation for the environment, and thoughtful consideration of the risks and safety of self and others.</p><ul><li><p><strong>National Physical Fitness Award (NAPFA)</strong></p></li></ul><p>This test is conducted for all Secondary 2, 4 and 5 students annually and encourages our students to stay physically fit through regular exercise.</p><p><br></p>

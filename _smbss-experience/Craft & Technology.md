<p><strong>About the Department</strong></p><p>Our Craft and Technology (C&amp;T) department is a close-knit team of dedicated teachers who selflessly and willingly contribute to creating engaging lessons in a conducive learning environment. We firmly believe that every child can learn and work tirelessly to ensure their success.</p><p></p><p>The mission of our C&amp;T department is to cultivate creative and innovative thinkers among our students who can lead with empathy. We promote experiential learning, encouraging students to reflect on their experiences and develop into independent, versatile learners who are adaptable to change.</p><p><strong>Learning opportunities for students</strong></p><p>To prepare our students for an increasingly complex, interconnected, and tech-driven world, our curriculum is designed to develop students' competencies in acquiring and applying knowledge (K) and skills (S) while nurturing positive attitudes (A) and values (V) to meet complex demands.

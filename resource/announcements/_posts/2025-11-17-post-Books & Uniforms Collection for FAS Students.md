<h4>If your child's FAS application is successful, the following dates will apply:</h4>
<p>If you have children in different level(s), you may choose to come on
any one of the allocated dates for the collection of books and uniforms.
Your child will only be able to use his/ her Student Smart Card (Ezlink
Card) to purchase food in the school canteen. Please ask your child to
bring his/her own Ezlink card to the General Office to activate for the
Meal Subsidy System on the same day.</p>

<h4>DSA at Sembawang Secondary School</h4>
<p>The school is inviting applications from current Primary 6 students for
DSA in the talent area of&nbsp;Leadership.</p>
<p>The school provides a structured leadership development programme that
offers opportunities for all students to develop their leadership at three
different levels which are ‘Leading Self, Leading Peers and Leading Leaders”.
Their leadership journey in the school includes opportunities for them
to Learn, Lead and Serve both in school and the community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Student Leaders in the school will be offered the following developmental
opportunities:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Learning about
what it takes to be an effective leader through The Leadership Challenge
model</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Assuming a key
leadership role such as Student Councilor, CCA Leader, Peer Support Leader
or Sports Leader</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Leading in planning
and supporting various key school programmes and events</p>
<p>Application Process</p>
<p>Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal.
The application is free-of-charge and will be open from&nbsp;<strong>6 May 2026, 11am to 2 Jun 2026, 4.30pm</strong>.</p>
<p>For more details, please refer to MOE website –&nbsp;<a href="http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>.</p>
<p>Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on
the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic
and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop
the area of talent.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are
required to honour their commitment to the school and participate in the
activities related to the talent they are selected for from year 1-4.</p>
<p>Selection Process</p>
<p>The selection criteria are listed below:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Applicants should be
passionate about serving others in school and community.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Applicants should demonstrate
leadership attributes such as positive role modelling and building a positive
team.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Applicants should have
some prior experience in Student Leadership in primary school, such as
Prefect, Peer Support Leader, Class Leader or CCA Leader.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Applicants who do not have prior experience may apply. The school will
assess applicants through an&nbsp;in-person interview&nbsp;based on the
selection criteria, which may include assessing the potential of the applicants.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Meeting all the criteria does not guarantee the student will be shortlisted/given
an offer.</p>
<p>Timeline</p>
<p>You will receive notification from the school <strong>by&nbsp;10 Jul 2026</strong>,
if your child has been shortlisted to undergo Direct School Admission (DSA)
selection.</p>
<p>All applicants will receive their application outcomes between&nbsp;<strong>17 Aug&nbsp;and 28 Aug 2026.</strong>
</p>

<p>The 2025 GCE O-Level results will be released on <strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2026. </strong>
</p>
<p>Students from Sec <strong>4D, Sec 4E, Sec 5A and students from Sec 4A, 4B and 4C</strong>,
who have sat for papers at the O-Level Exam, are to report to the school
hall by <strong>1.45pm on 14 January 2026</strong> to attend an important
briefing regarding post-secondary courses before collecting your result
slips and certificates.</p>
<p>Please <strong>report in full school uniform or PE T-shirt and trousers/skirt</strong> and
be in <strong>proper grooming</strong>. If you are feeling unwell on 14
January 2026, do not report to school. Inform your teachers and they will
provide you with more information.</p>
<p>Parents/Guardians can refer to the attached message and information sheet
on how you can better support your child/ward.</p>

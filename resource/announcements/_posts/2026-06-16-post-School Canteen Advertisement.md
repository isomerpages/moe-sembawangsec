<p>Sembawang Secondary School seeks applications to operate a Halal noodle
stall in the school canteen. If successful, operation of the new stall
will likely commence from September 2026.</p>
<p><strong><u>Application Criteria</u></strong>
<br>Applicants should possess the following criteria:
<br>a) Must be a Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident;
<br>b) Must possess Workforce Skills Qualification (WSQ) Food Safety Course
Level 1 (formerly known as Basic Food Hygiene Course (BFHC)) conducted
by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG)- approved training providers;
<br>c) Able to provide food/ drinks that meet the health/nutritional standards
under the Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP) set by the Health Promotion
Board (HPB) (<a href="https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme</a>);
<br>d) Able to provide good service and quality food/drinks at prices as recommended
by MOE;
<br>e) Able to observe a high standard of food/drinks and personal hygiene
as per Singapore Food Agency (SFA) requirements;
<br>f) Must be operating the stall in person during the specified operating
hours;</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Application Process</u></strong>
<br>Download and complete the application form below. You may also request
for a hardcopy from the School’s General Office at 9am – 5pm, Monday to
Friday.</p>
<p><u>Application Form</u>
</p>
<p>Attach the following supporting documents together with the application
form:
<br>a) A photocopy of applicant’s NRIC (front and back)
<br>b) A photocopy of applicant’s Certificate in Workforce Skills Qualification
(WSQ) Food Safety Course Level 1, and any other relevant certificates in
Food Hygiene.
<br>c) List of proposed menu and prices, with details in ingredients and portions
used for each item.
<br>d) Proof of previous experience in operating a food stall (at school canteen
or outside school), if applicable.</p>
<p>Submit the completed application form and supporting documents by closing
date via email (sembawang.ss@moe.gov.sg) OR to the School’s General Office
at 9am – 5pm, Monday to Friday.</p>
